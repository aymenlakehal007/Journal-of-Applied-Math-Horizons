About Our Journal

Committed to excellence in applied mathematics research

Our Mission

Advancing applied mathematics through rigorous research

The Journal of Applied Math Horizons is dedicated to publishing high-quality research in applied mathematics, computational methods, and interdisciplinary applications. Since our establishment in 1992, we have maintained the highest standards of mathematical scholarship.

Our editorial board comprises leading applied mathematicians from around the world, ensuring rigorous peer review and timely publication of groundbreaking research. We are committed to advancing mathematical knowledge and fostering collaboration among researchers globally.
