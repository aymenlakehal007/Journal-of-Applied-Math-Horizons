Submission Guidelines

Please follow our requirements for manuscript preparation

Manuscript Format

Submissions must be in LaTeX format using our journal template. The main text should be single-spaced with 1-inch margins.

Language

Articles must be written in clear, correct English. Non-native speakers are encouraged to have their manuscripts checked by a native speaker.

Structure

Manuscripts should include: Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Main Results, Proofs, Conclusion, and References.

Figures & Tables

All figures must be in high resolution (at least 300 dpi). Tables should be editable and not embedded in figures.

Review Process

Our peer review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. You will receive notification of acceptance, revision requests, or rejection.

2

3
